Burgers

Choose from our selection of hamburgers, chicken burgers or veggie burgers. Each burger is made to order using our fresh products to ensure satisfaction. Top your meal off with your favourite condiments, sauces and vegetables.

Grab the iconic Canadian meal made fresh from our kitchen. Freshly cooked fries topped with curd cheese and gravy. Customize your poutine with a variety of meats, vegetables or sauces. Plain fries are also available.

Our chicken comes in many forms. Pick your traditional favourite or try something new! Chicken fingers, nuggets, grilled chicken portions and more; there is sure to be something for everyone.
